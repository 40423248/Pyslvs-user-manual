其他試作範例:

1.羅伯特機構(Robert Mechaniam):

當(point0+point1)=(point1+point2)=(point2+point3)=(point3+point4),以及(point0+point2)=(point1+point3)=(point2+point4)時,point2可以做出近似於直線運動的路徑,而增加長度的比值時,會有更準確的直線運動

Pyslvs 0.9-beta.1 test Robert Mechaniam from 40423248 on Vimeo.

2.牽桿機構(Drag-link Mechanism):

此機構的最短桿為固定桿(point0+point3),曲柄(point0+point2),(point3+point4)可以做整圈的旋轉

Pyslvs 0.9-beta.1 test Drag-link Mechanism from 40423248 on Vimeo.

3.平行四連桿機構(Parallel crank four-bar linkage)

此機構的固定桿(point0+point3)與連接桿(point1+point2)一樣長,而兩個曲柄(point0+point1),(point2+point3)也一樣長,可以做旋轉運動

Pyslvs 0.9-beta.1 test Parallel crank four-bar linkage from 40423248 on Vimeo.

4.雙搖桿機構Double rocker mechanism

當兩根搖桿(point0+point2),(point1+point3)對固定桿(point0+point3)做往復搖擺運動,就會形成此機構

Pyslvs 0.9-beta.1 test Double rocker mechanism from 40423248 on Vimeo.
